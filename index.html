What is this?

This Twitter list contains all getmakerlog.com users who have filled out their Twitter Handle. Makerlog is a community of makers, entrepreneurs, hobbyists and people who like to create things and are productive.

Makerlog is the dead-simple task log that helps you stay productive and ship faster!

How do I get on this list?

  1. Register at Makerlog
  2. Fill out your Twitter handle
  3. Done!
